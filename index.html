    • What does a Director Do

    A director is responsible for overseeing the artistic and creative aspects of a production, from interpreting the script to guiding the performances of the actors. They collaborate with various departments to plan the visual elements of the production, including cinematography, set design, and costumes. During filming or performances, directors provide guidance and feedback to the cast to ensure their vision is realized on screen or stage. They make critical decisions throughout the production process and are ultimately accountable for the final artistic product.
